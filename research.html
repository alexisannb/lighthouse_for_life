Lighthouse for Life uses their social media platforms to educate about human trafficking and related topics. They also use their online platform to advertise for support-raising events, such as their annual 5K. Additionally, they have training and volunteer opportunities in the organization.

Lighthouse for Life is consistently visually portrayed by bold, all-caps text and a color palette of blue, orange, and purple.
