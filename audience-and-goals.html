Audience

Defining the Audience.


The people who Lighthouse For Life aim to help are those who are victims of human trafficking, so all who are involved in the business of human trafficking are included as the organization's audience. The remaining audience for Lighthouse For Life is broad, because one of their main goals is to educate as many people as possible about the realities of human trafficking. However, there are particular groups of people whose awareness is of special importance, such as law enforcement, prosecutors, and any workers in the community who may have increased chances of encountering human trafficking activity. Local businesses, churches, and agencies are included because they are potential partners and sponsors for the organization. Additionally, youth, teens, parents, and schools are important audiences to educate about human trafficking. Potential volunteers are named as well, which can include anyone, although college students are a likely audience.

Goals

Determining the Goals of Lightouse For Life and their Website and its Users.


Client Goals


Spread as much information about human trafficking as possible to as many people as possible.

Share who they are as an organization and what they are doing.

Provide assistance for those leaving the life of human trafficking.



Audience Goals


Find information about Lighthouse For Life.

Gain education about human trafficking.

Find ways to help fight against human trafficking, donate, or volunteer with Lighthouse For Life.



Website Goals


Provide a place where people can find updated information about the organization.

Educate people about human trafficking.

Provide resources for those who need help or have questions.

Connect with people who want to volunteer their time or support them financially.
